What is Spite & Stack?

A compact, quick, dangerously competitive tabletop game. Build unstable towers, sabotage opponents, and laugh when everything collapses.

Pick and place pieces to build your unstable masterpiece.

2. Balance

Steady hands, risky choices. Height matters.

3. Spite

Spend sticks to sabotage. No mercy, only physics.

4. Score

After 10 rounds, the tallest (and sneakiest) wins.
